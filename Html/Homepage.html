About Us

Who we are?

The WITS Promaths Society is a vibrant student-led community formed by alumni of the Promaths program, united by a shared mission to uplift, connect, and empower one another throughout university and beyond. As one of the many Promaths chapters across South Africa, our Wits branch focuses on cultivating academic excellence, leadership, and lifelong connections among our 500+ members.

Our Mission

To empower university students through peer support, academic enrichment, networking opportunities, and leadership development — building a strong legacy of Promaths excellence.

Our Vision

A united Promaths community that leads with purpose, excels in diverse fields, and gives back meaningfully to the next generation.

What We Do:

Networking

We host networking events with Promaths alumni and industry leaders who share insights, advice, and career guidance.

Collaboration

We collaborate with Investec, other Wits societies, and Promaths chapters at other universities to expand opportunities and build a national network.

Giving Back

We run outreach visits to high schools, motivational talks, and mentorship for current Promaths learners, strengthening the pipeline for future students.

Mentorship

We facilitate mentorship between senior students and juniors, as well as alumni connections, to guide members through academic and personal development.

Academics

We organize study sessions, peer-led tutorials, and academic discussions to help students excel in their respective degrees.

Big Events

We organize events like Braais, Game Nights, Movie Nights, Gala Dinner where we aim to have fun and try to help our member distress from all that's happening in their varsity life.
